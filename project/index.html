Automate The Boring Stuff With Python

I am going to review the book Automate the Boring stuff with python. I will be comparing this book with other books on python I hd read. I will give an opinion on wether or not its a good book to introduce to my kids or not.

The book is written by Al Sweigart. I will be reviewing the useability and how well he breaks it down.

I found Al Sweigarts book Cracking Codes with Python significantly easier to read compared to Automate the Boring stuff. The book seems to skip ahead in the learning process significantly fast without proper explaining at all. In addition to this I've come to have problems with allot of the code he wrote in this book. Often leading to me needing to have co-pilot completely re-write the code for me. I would recommend his other book Cracking codes with Python as well as Eric Matthes book "Python crash Course" much better books to read that do much a much better job at breaking things down as well as introducing to my kids.

Al Sweigart has a website where you can read some of hss books for free https://inventwithpython.com/
